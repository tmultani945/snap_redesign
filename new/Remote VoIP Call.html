▸ What happens when I press Start?

RabbitMQ sends a DIAL command to the external device's .xperf-testing queue. AWS Connect routes the inbound call to the assigned DID. We watch xperf-call-states for connected → disconnect transitions. Artifacts upload to S3 after the run.
